4. Educational Interaction

During the visit, I explained the QRS complex, which consists of three graphical deflections on an electrocardiogram (ECG) that represent ventricular depolarization. Understanding the QRS complex is crucial for diagnosing various cardiac conditions, including arrhythmias and myocardial infarction.
